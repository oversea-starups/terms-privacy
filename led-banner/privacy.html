Privacy Policy

For LED Banner. Last updated: 2026-07-14.

LED Banner operates LED Banner. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, disclosed, and protected when you use the app, website, or related services.

Information We Collect

  • Core interaction events using predefined identifiers and categories; banner text and other user-created content are excluded.
  • Crash and diagnostic information such as stack traces, app state, device model, operating-system version, installation identifiers, and session metadata.
  • Verified StoreKit transaction values used for aggregate purchase measurement.
  • Purchase, subscription, or entitlement information processed through app stores or payment providers.
  • Banner text and display settings stored only on the user's device.

We collect information only as needed to provide, maintain, secure, and improve LED Banner.

How We Use Information

  • Provide and operate the service.
  • Maintain security, prevent abuse, and troubleshoot issues.
  • Respond to support, privacy, or legal requests.
  • Improve features, performance, and user experience.
  • Process purchases or subscriptions when applicable.

Third-Party Services

LED Banner uses the following third-party services for app distribution, subscriptions, limited analytics, and crash diagnostics. It does not use advertising or cross-app tracking services.

  • Apple App Store and StoreKit
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Crashlytics

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, app stores, legal authorities when required, or as part of a business transfer, only as permitted by applicable law.

Data Retention

Locally saved signs remain on the device until the user deletes them or removes the app. Apple controls subscription-record retention. Firebase Analytics and Crashlytics data follow the retention settings and controls provided by the associated Google and Firebase services.

Your Choices And Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance by contacting us. Some rights depend on your location and applicable law.

Children

LED Banner is not directed to children under 13 unless explicitly stated in the service. If you believe a child provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate action.

International Users

Your information may be processed in countries other than where you live. We use reasonable safeguards where required by applicable law.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last-updated date.
