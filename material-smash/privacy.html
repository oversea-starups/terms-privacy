Material Smash Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 22, 2026

1. Overview

Material Smash ("we", "our", or "this app") is a haptic-first ASMR destruction game developed natively for iOS. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have.

Key Principle: Material Smash does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All game progress is stored locally on your device.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we don't do:

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any personal identifier
  • We do not require an account or login
  • We do not track your location
  • We do not use analytics SDKs
  • We do not sell or share any data with third parties
  • We do not access your photos, contacts, microphone, or camera

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored only on your device using iOS's built-in UserDefaults system. It is never sent to any server:

  • Game Progress: Best score, total objects smashed, highest level reached, level stars
  • Shards Currency: Shards earned and spent in-game
  • Material Collection: Which materials you have discovered
  • Purchase Status: Whether you have purchased "Remove Ads"
  • Settings: Sound, haptic intensity, and gameplay preferences

This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

4. Apple Game Center

Material Smash integrates with Apple's Game Center for leaderboards. When you submit a score:

  • The score is sent directly to Apple's servers via GameKit framework
  • Apple handles all authentication and data processing under Apple's Privacy Policy
  • We do not receive or store any Game Center data
  • You can disable Game Center in your device Settings at any time

5. In-App Purchases

Material Smash offers a one-time "Remove Ads" purchase ($1.99 USD) processed by Apple's StoreKit framework:

  • All payment processing is handled by Apple under their Privacy Policy
  • We only receive a notification that the purchase was successful
  • Your purchase status is stored locally and synced via your Apple ID

6. Advertising

Material Smash may display advertisements, including banner ads, interstitial ads, and rewarded video ads. When an advertising SDK (such as Google AdMob) is integrated:

  • You will be shown an App Tracking Transparency (ATT) prompt before any personalized ads are served
  • If you decline tracking, you will still see ads, but they will not be personalized
  • You can remove all ads permanently by purchasing "Remove Ads"
  • The advertising SDK may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in their privacy policy

7. Haptic Feedback & Audio

The app generates haptic feedback using Apple's Core Haptics framework and audio using AVAudioEngine. All sounds are synthesized at runtime with no audio files downloaded. These features:

  • Operate entirely on-device
  • Do not collect or transmit any data
  • Can be disabled in the app's Settings screen

8. Children's Privacy

Material Smash is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13. No account creation is required. If we add any features in the future that involve data collection, we will update this policy and obtain appropriate consent.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no data to access, correct, or delete on our servers. To erase all locally stored data, simply uninstall the app.

10. Third-Party Links

This app may link to Apple's Game Center and App Store. These services have their own privacy policies that you should review.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

12. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@materialsmash.app
