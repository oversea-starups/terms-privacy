❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What image formats does Image Converter support?
Image Converter supports conversion between the following formats:
  • Input formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, HEIC, HEIF, BMP, TIFF, GIF, WebP, PDF
  • Output formats: JPG, PNG, WebP, PDF, HEIC
We continuously add support for new formats. If you need a specific format, let us know via a feature request.
Will image quality be compressed during conversion?
You have full control over output quality:
  • JPG format: adjustable compression quality (1–100%)
  • PNG format: lossless compression, preserves original quality
  • WebP format: choose between lossy or lossless compression
  • HEIC format: maintains high quality while reducing file size
Default settings strike a good balance between quality and file size.
Will my images be uploaded to a server?
Absolutely not. Image Converter uses a fully local processing architecture:
  • All conversions are performed on your device
  • No internet connection is required to use the app
  • Your images never leave your device
  • We follow a privacy-first design philosophy
Your data is completely under your control.
Does it support batch conversion?
Yes! Image Converter supports powerful batch conversion:
  • Select multiple images at once
  • Apply the same conversion settings to all images
  • Batch export to Photos or Files
  • Maintain original file organization
The free version has a limit on batch size. Upgrade to Lifetime Pro for unlimited batch conversions.
Where are converted images saved?
You can choose where to save your images:
  • Photos: Save directly to the iOS photo library
  • Files: Save to iCloud Drive or local files
  • Share: Share directly to other apps
You can set a default save location in Settings, or choose each time you convert.
What is Lifetime Pro?
Lifetime Pro is a one-time purchase ($9.99) that unlocks all premium features permanently:
  • Unlimited batch conversions
  • All output formats (including HEIC)
  • Advanced quality settings
  • Custom output dimensions
  • No recurring charges
  • Priority technical support
Once purchased, all features are unlocked forever with no subscription fees.
Can I restore my purchase?
Yes! You can restore your Lifetime Pro purchase on any device:
  1. Open the Image Converter app
  2. Tap the Settings gear icon
  3. Tap "Restore Purchase"
This will verify your purchase with Apple and unlock all Pro features on your current device.
What should I do if the app crashes?
If the app crashes, try the following steps:
  1. Fully close the app (remove it from the app switcher) and reopen it
  2. Restart your device
  3. Check for app updates
  4. Check for iOS system updates
  5. Delete and reinstall the app (note: this will not delete any saved images)
If the issue persists, please contact our support team with your device model and iOS version.
Which iOS versions are supported?
Image Converter supports iOS 15.0 and later.

For the best experience, we recommend:
  • Using the latest iOS version
  • Keeping the app updated to the latest version
  • Ensuring your device has sufficient storage space
Is there a free version?
Yes! Image Converter offers a free version with the following features:
  • Basic conversion between JPG, PNG, and PDF formats
  • Batch processing (up to 3 photos per batch)
  • Standard quality settings
  • All core conversion features
Upgrade to Lifetime Pro for unlimited batches and all formats.
How do I request a refund?
Refund requests must be processed through Apple:
  1. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com
  2. Sign in with your Apple ID
  3. Find the Image Converter purchase
  4. Tap "Report a Problem"
  5. Select a refund reason and submit
Apple's standard refund policy applies. If you have questions, you can also contact Apple Support directly.

📖 User Guide

🔄 Quick Start: Convert a Single Image

  1. Open the Image Converter app
  2. Tap the "+" button or "Select Image" on the main screen
  3. Choose the image you want to convert from your photo library
  4. Select the target format (JPG, PNG, WebP, or PDF)
  5. Adjust quality and size settings (optional)
  6. Tap the "Convert" button
  7. Choose a save location (Photos or Files)

📦 Batch Convert Multiple Images

  1. Tap the "Batch Convert" option
  2. Select multiple images (swipe to multi-select)
  3. Confirm your selection and tap "Next"
  4. Set a unified output format and quality
  5. Tap "Start Batch Conversion"
  6. Wait for processing to complete
  7. Save or share the converted images in bulk

⚙️ Custom Conversion Settings

  1. Tap the Settings gear icon on the conversion screen
  2. Adjust the output quality slider (JPG/WebP only)
  3. Set output dimensions (Original, Custom, or Preset)
  4. Choose the default save location
  5. Toggle the option to delete the original after conversion
  6. Settings are automatically saved and applied to future conversions

📤 Export & Share

  1. Tap the preview after conversion is complete
  2. Tap the share button
  3. Choose a sharing destination (social media, email, messages, etc.)
  4. Or use "Save to Files" to export to a specific location
  5. Supports AirDrop for quick transfer to a Mac or other devices

🔧 Troubleshooting

Conversion Fails or Freezes

The image may be corrupted or in an unsupported format

Solution: Try selecting a different image, or first convert the image to a common format (such as JPG) and try again.

Save to Photos Failed

The app may not have write permission for the photo library

Solution: Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > Image Converter, and make sure the permission is set to "All Photos."

Cannot Select Images

The app may not have access to the photo library

Solution: Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Photos > Image Converter, and grant "All Photos" or "Selected Photos" permission.

Output File Too Large

The quality setting may be too high

Solution: Lower the quality setting for JPG/WebP (recommended 70–85%), or use WebP/HEIC format for better compression.

Batch Conversion Interrupted

Insufficient device storage or the app was sent to the background

Solution: Make sure your device has enough storage, keep the app in the foreground during conversion, or process images in smaller batches.

HEIC Conversion Failed

Pro feature or iOS version not supported

Solution: Make sure you have purchased Lifetime Pro and are using iOS 15+. HEIC output is only supported as an input format on iOS 11–14.
