🔐 Core Privacy Promise

Your images are always processed locally on your device and are never uploaded to any server. We adopt a privacy-first design philosophy to ensure your personal data remains entirely under your control.

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy applies to the Image Converter application (hereinafter referred to as "the App"), operated by an independent developer. We place great importance on user privacy and are committed to explaining in a transparent manner how we collect, use, and protect your information.

By using the App, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the App.

2. Information We Collect

We adhere to the principle of data minimization and collect only the minimum information necessary for the App to function properly:

Data Type Purpose Personally Identifiable
App Interaction Data Improving product features and user experience No (anonymized)
Device Information Diagnosing technical issues and optimizing performance No
Crash Logs Fixing application errors No

2.1 Information We Do Not Collect

For the avoidance of doubt, we never collect the following information:

  • The content of images you convert
  • Your name, address, phone number, or other personally identifiable information
  • Precise location information
  • Contact information
  • Payment information (all purchases are processed through Apple)

3. Photo Library Permission

The App requires access to your photo library to provide its core functionality. Our use of permissions is as follows:

📷 Photo Library Read Access

Used to select images for format conversion. We only read images you actively select and do not scan or upload your entire photo library.

💾 Photo Library Write Access

Used to save converted images to your photo library. Converted results are written to the library only after your confirmation.

Important Notice: All image processing is completed locally on your device. Your original and converted images never leave your device.

4. Data Storage and Processing

4.1 Local Processing Principle

Image Converter employs a fully local processing architecture:

  • All image format conversions are performed on your device
  • No internet connection is required to use core features
  • Images are not uploaded to cloud servers
  • Temporary files created during conversion are stored only in the device's local sandbox

4.2 Temporary Files

The App may generate temporary files during conversion. These files:

  • Are saved only within the App's sandbox directory
  • Are automatically cleaned up after conversion is complete
  • Are completely deleted when the App is uninstalled
  • Cannot be accessed by other applications

5. Third-Party Services

Image Converter does not integrate any third-party analytics SDKs or advertising SDKs. All data remains on your device locally and is not transmitted to third-party services.

6. Your Rights

Depending on the laws and regulations in your jurisdiction, you may be entitled to the following rights:

6.1 GDPR (Users in the European Union)

  • Right to Access: Obtain information about what data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (the "right to be forgotten")
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Restrict the processing of your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Obtain your data in a commonly used format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of data processing

6.2 CCPA (Users in California)

  • Know the categories and specific pieces of personal information we collect
  • Know the categories of sources from which personal information is collected
  • Know the purposes for which personal information is collected
  • Know the categories of third parties with whom we share personal information
  • Request deletion of personal information we have collected
  • Not be discriminated against for exercising your privacy rights

6.3 How to Exercise Your Rights

Since we collect only a minimal amount of anonymized data, most user rights requests can be fulfilled as follows:

  • Reset your advertising identifier in iOS Settings
  • Uninstall the App to delete all local data
  • Contact us using the information below for specific requests

We will respond to all reasonable rights requests within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we may have collected information from a child, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to delete such information.

8. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:

  • Using iOS standard sandbox mechanisms to protect App data
  • Following Apple's security best practices
  • Regularly updating the App to fix security vulnerabilities
  • Using iOS standard security mechanisms to protect App data
