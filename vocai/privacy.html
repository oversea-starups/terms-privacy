Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 8, 2026

1. Overview

Vocai ("we", "our", or "the App") is an AI-powered voice notes application developed for iOS. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle your data.

Key Privacy Principles:
  • Your audio recordings are processed temporarily for transcription and never used for AI training
  • We use Apple Sign In for authentication — we do not collect passwords
  • Notes are stored locally on your device with optional iCloud sync
  • We do not sell your personal data to third parties
  • No third-party advertising or tracking

2. Data We Collect

2.1 Audio Data

When you record a voice note, audio data is:

  • Stored locally on your device
  • Sent to our servers temporarily for AI transcription and organization using OpenAI services
  • Not retained by our servers after processing is complete
  • Not used for AI model training or any purpose beyond your requested transcription

2.2 Account Information

When you sign in with Apple, we may receive:

  • Your Apple ID identifier (for authentication)
  • Your email address (only if you choose to share it)
  • Your name (only if you choose to share it)

2.3 Note Content

Your notes — including transcriptions, AI-organized content, titles, categories, and tags — are:

  • Stored locally on your device using SwiftData
  • Synchronized to our server database for backup and cross-device access
  • Optionally synced via iCloud (CloudKit) when enabled

2.4 Usage and Analytics Data

We collect anonymous usage data to improve the App, including:

  • Feature usage patterns (recording duration, templates used)
  • AI processing success/failure events
  • Device type and iOS version
  • App performance and stability metrics

This data is anonymous and cannot be used to identify you personally.

2.5 Device Information

We collect limited device information for security and subscription management:

  • Device identifier (for device limit enforcement)
  • Device model and OS version

4. Third-Party Services

4.1 OpenAI

We use OpenAI's API for transcription (Whisper) and text organization (GPT). Audio and text data are sent to OpenAI for processing. OpenAI does not use this data to train their models. OpenAI Privacy Policy

4.2 Apple Sign In

Authentication is handled through Apple's Sign in with Apple framework. We receive only the information you choose to share. Apple Privacy Policy

4.3 Apple iCloud (CloudKit)

When you enable iCloud sync, your notes are encrypted and stored in your iCloud account by Apple. We do not have direct access to your iCloud data. Apple Privacy Policy

4.4 Apple StoreKit (In-App Purchases)

All subscription purchases are processed by Apple. We do not have access to your full payment information. We only receive transaction receipts for verification. Apple Privacy Policy

4.5 Apple Speech Framework

Real-time speech recognition during recording is processed by Apple's Speech framework, either on-device or via Apple's servers depending on your device capabilities.

5. Data Storage & Security

  • Local Storage: Notes and preferences stored on your device using SwiftData and Keychain
  • Server Storage: Encrypted database (MySQL) with encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Caching: Redis for performance optimization, with automatic expiration
  • Authentication: JWT tokens stored securely in iOS Keychain
  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.3

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion — Delete your account and all associated data at any time via Settings → Account → Delete Account
  • Export — Request export of your notes and data
  • Withdraw Consent — Disable iCloud sync or analytics in Settings

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at support@vocai.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Vocai is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such data, we will take steps to delete it.

9. International Data Transfers

Your data may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place, including encryption and compliance with applicable data protection laws (GDPR, CCPA).

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you by:

  • Updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page
  • Posting a notice in the App for significant changes

11. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: support@vocai.app
  • Security: security@vocai.app
  • Response Time: Within 24 hours (business days)

Summary: Vocai respects your privacy. We process your audio temporarily for transcription, store your notes securely, use Apple's built-in services for authentication and payments, and never sell your data to third parties.
