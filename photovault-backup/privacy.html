Privacy Policy

For PhotoVault Backup. Last updated: 2026-07-22.

Beijing baqijiuyi Technology Co., Ltd provides PhotoVault Backup. PhotoVault is a local, read-only Photos export utility. It has no account, advertising, analytics, tracking, developer-operated export server, or in-app purchase in version 1.0.

Data We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your photos, videos, Photos-library metadata, original filenames, locations, albums, export manifests, job history, checksums, destination paths, security-scoped bookmarks, or diagnostic packages. The app does not sell personal information or track you across apps or websites.

Photos Access

PhotoVault requests Photos read access only to scan and export items available under the permission you grant in iOS. Limited Access limits the app to the items you select. PhotoVault does not delete, edit, hide, or reorganize your Photos library, and version 1.0 does not import an export package back into Photos.

Information Stored Locally

Job checkpoints, resource identifiers, error history, settings, and local diagnostic records stay in the app container. Exported media, available filename and date metadata, per-resource manifests, checksums, and reports are written to the folder or drive you select. Exported media may retain metadata embedded by its source. You control and are responsible for protecting the destination.

Network And Apple Services

PhotoVault does not upload export content to us. When an original is stored in iCloud Photos rather than locally on the iPhone, Apple Photos and iCloud Photos may use the network to deliver it under your Apple settings and relationship with Apple. Apple also distributes the app through the App Store. The app includes no third-party advertising or analytics SDK.

Diagnostics And Support

PhotoVault can create a diagnostic package locally. By default it excludes media, thumbnails, original filenames, raw Photos identifiers, locations, album names, raw destination paths, and security-scoped bookmark data. Creating a package does not send it to us; it leaves the device only if you deliberately share it.

If you email support, we receive the address, message, attachments, and other information you choose to provide. We use that correspondence only to respond, troubleshoot, protect the app, and comply with law. We do not sell it and retain it only as reasonably necessary for those purposes and legal obligations. You may request deletion by email where applicable.

Retention And Deletion

Local job history and settings remain until you clear supported history, delete the app, or iOS removes app data. Diagnostic and export packages remain where you save them until you delete them. Clearing app history or deleting PhotoVault does not delete packages stored outside the app container.

Your Choices And Rights

You can deny or limit Photos access, change it in iOS Settings, choose the destination, pause or cancel a job, clear supported local history, and decide whether to create or share diagnostics. For support information you voluntarily sent, you may request access, correction, or deletion where applicable by contacting us.

Security And Integrity Limits

PhotoVault uses iOS sandboxing, system-selected file access, local persistence, and SHA-256 destination rereads to reduce accidental errors. No storage medium or software can be guaranteed permanently secure or error-free. A verification report confirms only the checks stated in that report at completion. Keep your Photos originals and independently test important files.

Children And International Use

PhotoVault is a general utility and is not directed to children under 13. Photos processing occurs on your device and selected destination. Support information you voluntarily send may be processed where the developer and the email services you choose operate, subject to applicable law.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be posted on this page with a new last-updated date.
