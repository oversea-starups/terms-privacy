We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, app stores, legal authorities when required, or as part of a business transfer, only as permitted by applicable law.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as needed for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required by law, security, dispute resolution, or legitimate business needs.

Your Choices And Rights

You may request access, correction, deletion, or other privacy assistance by contacting us. Some rights depend on your location and applicable law.

International Users
